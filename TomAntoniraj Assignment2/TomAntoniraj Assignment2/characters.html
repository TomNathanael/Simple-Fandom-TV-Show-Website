MAIN Protaganists

David Martinez

David Martinez is the one of the two protagonist of Cyberpunk: Edgerunners.
He became a mercenary and a member of Maine's edgerunner crew in 2076,
when he was involved in a series of harrowing events that resulted in him attaining the status of a Night City legend.

Lucy Kushinada

Lucy is a level-headed, street-smart netrunner with a top-tier sense of self-preservation. She is the second protangist.
She is responsible for recruiting the lead character into the Edgerunner crew. She and David also have a Love relationship which carries on to the end of the season also leading into a tragic ending.

Supporting Characters

Kiwi

Kiwi is an enigmatic and snarky netrunner with a detachable cybernetic jaw mask who acts as a mentor to Lucy.
Kiwi later become a major antagonist towards the end. She builds up a lot of hate towards the main character David which explains her betrayal.

Dorio

Dorio is an agile and enigmatic character who can destroy her enemies with her massive assault rifle,
which is a variant of the Masamune from the game. She can also rip her opponents to shreds using her Gorilla Arms augmentation.
She also has a Loving Relationship with Maine the leader of the gang which doesnt end too well for them either.

Rebecca

Rebecca is a character in the Cyberpunk: Edgerunners anime. She is introduced as the younger sister of a fellow edgerunner named Pilar.
Rebecca is portrayed as a pretty violent person, never wanting to back down in a fight and always at the forefront of the battle with no remorse.
She is loved by viewers for her adorable design and hot-blooded personality.

Maine

He is a veteran edgerunner who commands his own crew. Maine was the acting leader when David joined his gang of edgerunners.
He was the original owner of the Sandevistan implant that David had installed on himself. Maine purchased the implant from Gloria,
who died before fulfilling her end of the bargain. He has a Loving Relationship with Dorio.

Falco

Falco serves as the primary driver for Maine's crew. He plays a minor yet critical role in the side mission.
Falco is known for his strength, mental toughness, and signature weapon, the Techtronika RT-46 Burya.
He helps V find David’s jacket, although the two never meet physically.

Antagonist

Faraday

Faraday was a corporate fixer and the main antagonist of Cyberpunk: Edgerunners. Faraday worked for Militech and can be described as a vicious and unmerciful fixer.
His main goal was to take down Maine's crew and kill them along with David.
He will also appear in the Cyberpunk 2077: Gangs of Night City board game.
